Siglap secondary school

BOD is Singapore’s largest creative outlaw gang on a mission to rob the world of blank spaces and bland designs. Formerly known as Band of Doodlers, we are now known variously but not exclusively as Band of Doodlers, Designers, Disruptors, Dreamers… you get the picture.

We are over 200 art bandits strong – drawing, designing and doodling all kinds of fun. From illustration to graphic design, animation to motion graphics, we have the complete arsenal of all your visual storytelling needs in one posse.

It all began way back in 2013 when a guy called Mas, our founder and a compulsive doodler, started making monochromatic murals with a ragtag bunch of marker-wielding wildlings. Though not a single nib kissed a wall without a landlord’s permission, there was something inherently liberating about doing exactly what our parents told us never to do – draw on walls. It was sanctioned mischief; it was naughtiness made nice; it was fantastic iconoclastic manual gymnastics.

Mural Lingo is a multi-disciplinary platform-studio that works at the intersection of technology and arts. We aim to create immersive artistic experiences by using a combination of cutting edge technology and captivating mural paintings. Founded in 2014, we have worked with organisations such as Google, Red Cross, DBS, Deliveroo, Temasek Holdings, among others with whom we have developed private commissions, workshops, live events and CSR activities. As a socially and environmentally conscious enterprise in Singapore, we reinvest a proportion of our profits into organising projects and supporting initiatives that encourage the use of art as a tool to raise awareness and promote critical dialogue on key societal issues.
